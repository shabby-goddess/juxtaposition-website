## Cameron
Cameron DePutter is a queer and trans storyteller. An avid zine-maker since the '90s, a lifelong diarist, and a lover of making stuff, Cameron has been writing since he could hold a pencil, and self-publishing since he could staple paper together. In his storytelling business, Cameron uses his communications skills to help people express themselves effectively and authentically. He is the author of two books, Share Your Story: A Workbook, and Little Poems for Big Hearts. ## Vale
Vale is a weirdo from the suburbs. They write poetry about small happinesses, being mixed-race, mental illness, and the making of community. By day they work in office admin. By night they suffer from Does Too Many Things Syndrome. You can read their poetry in Rest for Resistance, Room, FEELS Zine, Living Hyphen, and QT Lit Mag. You can read their anime reviews on Wordpress. And you can occasionally see them tell jokes at open mics.
